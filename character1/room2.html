Blanket on the Floor: Given the space being quite tight, I imagine her sitting on the floor to do her makeup, but she would not want to sit on the hard wooden floor so putting a blanket down makes it more comfortable for her.
Makeup: As I said earlier I wanted the space to look lived in so on the table my her mirror is where she keeps her makeup, perfume, etc.. But I liked the idea of leaving some items open looking like they are still being used, others sticking out of the makeup bag and some just laying on the side. I felt like this gave the room a more realistic feel.
Mirror: A full length mirror is a must for a girls bedroom, It is a space where they can do their makeup, check their outfits and take hundreds of mirror selfies. With uni rooms often being quite small, I thought it did not make sense to have a desk space and a dresser so I opted for this make-shift dressing area where she can sit and do her makeup but also check out her outfit.
