Dresser: Doris has a dresser where she gets ready in the morning. It has a mirror and a space for her jewellery. She also has lots of trinkets on here and more pictures of her family.
Lace: Doris doesn't want the dresser to get scratched so one time when she was at the charity shop she brought this piece of lace to cover her dresser to keep the surface safe. She also thought that it looked pretty so it was a win win.
Photos: Doris loves having photos around her room as she is very family orientated. She has her favourite wedding photo on her bedside table and picture of her grandchildren dotted around her room.
Trinkets: Nan's love their trinkets and Doris is no different. Dotted across her room are little trinkets she has found in the charity shop.
